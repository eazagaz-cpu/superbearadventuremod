No. Root access would give the app full system control — a serious security risk that makes devices significantly more vulnerable. The Super Bear Adventure Mod APK is a **non-root mod**.

- No Magisk required
- No SuperSU or Kingroot
- No custom recovery (TWRP)
- No system partition modifications
- No Developer Options changes (beyond enabling unknown sources for install)

Standard Android security protections remain fully active after installation.
