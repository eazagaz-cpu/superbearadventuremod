Super Bear Fighters is the PvP mini-game added to Super Bear Adventure in v12.1.0 (November 2025). Located in the Arcade World, it is a fully functional fighting game mode where Baaren faces off against other players' bears in 1v1 and 2v2 matches.

## What Is Super Bear Fighters?

Super Bear Fighters takes the bear character models from Super Bear Adventure and puts them in a dedicated fighting arena. It is inspired by classic fighting games but simplified for casual play:

- 2D arena fighting on scrolling stages
- Health bars for both players
- Special moves unique to each bear skin
- Ranked matchmaking with seasonal rewards
- 1v1 and 2v2 game modes

This is not a separate game — it lives inside Super Bear Adventure's Arcade World and is accessible from the main game.

## How to Access Super Bear Fighters

1. From the Super Bear Adventure main menu, select **World Select**
2. Choose **Arcade World**
3. In the Arcade World, look for the Super Bear Fighters arena (large illuminated cabinet near the center)
4. Walk up to it and press the Interact button
5. Select 1v1 or 2v2, then Online Match or Local Match

**Requirement:** At least one world boss must be defeated to unlock the Arcade World.

## Game Modes

### 1v1 Online

Standard competitive mode. Matched against random opponents from the global player pool. Wins earn ranking points (see Ranking section).

### 2v2 Online

Team mode. Requires a partner from your friend list or current co-op session. Fight against another team of 2. Team matches earn 1.5x ranking points compared to 1v1.

### Local Match

Fight against a friend in the same co-op session. No ranking points are earned but it is great for practice. Both players need to be in the same multiplayer lobby. See the [multiplayer guide](/blog/multiplayer-guide/) for lobby setup.

### Training Mode

Practice mode with a stationary AI opponent. No ranking, no penalties. The only mode that works fully offline.

## Characters and Moves

Each bear skin in Super Bear Adventure has a slightly different moveset in Super Bear Fighters. The base moveset is the same for all bears but each has one unique special:

### Base Moveset (All Bears)

| Input | Move |
|-------|------|
| A/D (left/right) | Move |
| W / Tap Jump | Jump |
| Tap Attack | Jab (fast, low damage) |
| Hold Attack + release | Charged Strike (slow, high damage) |
| Down + Attack while airborne | Pounce (slam, good range) |
| Double tap direction | Dash |
| Dash + Attack | Dash Strike (moves forward, medium damage) |

### Special Moves by Skin

**Standard Baaren:** Spin Attack — 360-degree AoE around Baaren, hits both sides.

**Skeleton Bear:** Bone Toss — throws a projectile straight ahead. Only projectile in the game.

**Royal Cape:** Cape Slash — dramatic downward slash, highest single-hit damage of any move.

**Christmas Baaren:** Gift Drop — drops a present that explodes after 2 seconds. Can be placed strategically.

The mod APK unlocks all skins (see [unlock guide](/blog/unlock-all-skins-hats/)), giving you access to all special moves from the start.

## Ranking System

Super Bear Fighters has a seasonal ranking ladder:

### Ranks

1. **Honey Cub** — starting rank
2. **Bee Catcher** — intermediate
3. **Forest Guardian** — advanced
4. **Alpha Bear** — elite
5. **Earthkwak Champion** — highest rank, top players per season

### Ranking Points

- 1v1 win: +20 points
- 1v1 loss: −10 points
- 2v2 win: +30 points
- 2v2 loss: −10 points

Promotion to the next rank requires reaching a point threshold. Demotion happens if you fall below the rank floor.

### Seasonal Rewards

Each season (approximately 3 months) awards cosmetic rewards based on your rank at season end:

- Honey Cub: Standard season sticker
- Bee Catcher: Special hat
- Forest Guardian: Rare skin tone variant
- Alpha Bear: Exclusive seasonal skin
- Earthkwak Champion: Animated crown accessory (rarest cosmetic in the game)

## Strategies to Win

### For Beginners

1. **Learn the Charged Strike timing** — this is your highest-damage reliable move. Most new players only jab, which does low damage. Mixing Charged Strikes into your neutral game massively increases damage output.

2. **Use the Pounce attack** — the airborne pounce has the largest hitbox of any grounded move. Jump, then pounce to start most combos.

3. **Stay near the center** — falling off the arena edges results in ring-out (instant KO in some stages). Stay center until you learn stage layouts.

### Intermediate Play

1. **Match your skin's special to your playstyle:**
   - Skeleton Bear Bone Toss for zoning (keep distance)
   - Standard Baaren Spin for escape (use when surrounded)
   - Royal Cape slash for punishing dodges

2. **Watch for Dash Attack opportunities** — when opponents commit to a Charged Strike animation (it has a wind-up), dash in for a free punish.

3. **In 2v2, focus one target** — split damage between two opponents is inefficient. Both teammates should target the same opponent for faster KOs.

### Advanced

1. **Pounce-cancel combo:** Jump → Pounce → land → immediately Jab → Charged Strike. This 3-hit combo deals significant damage and the timing is fast enough that many opponents cannot interrupt it.

2. **Stage awareness:** Some Arcade World stages have moving platforms. Plan your movement around platform timing, not just opponent position.

3. **Read opponent specials:** Once you recognize which skin your opponent is using, you know their special move. Skeleton Bear players will try to zone — get in close. Royal Cape players will bait you to attack — bait their big swing instead.
