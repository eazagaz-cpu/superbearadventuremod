Emotes are Baaren's personality expression system — animations that show waves, dances, reactions, and victory poses. Added in v12.1.0, they brought personality to both solo exploration and multiplayer co-op sessions. This guide covers every emote, how to unlock each one, and tips for using them effectively.

## Emote System Overview

Emotes work via a quick-slot system:

- You have 4 quick slots for emotes
- Any unlocked emote can be assigned to any slot
- During gameplay, press the Emote button to open the 4-slot radial
- Select the emote to perform it
- Emote animations run for 2–5 seconds then Baaren returns to idle

The emote radial can be opened in any situation — during exploration, in combat, in boss rooms, and in multiplayer sessions.

## How to Unlock Emotes

### Standard Emotes (Coins)

Standard emotes are purchased with yellow coins in the Emote Shop (Accessories menu → Emotes). Prices range from 200 to 1,000 coins per emote.

### Premium Emotes (Purple Gems)

Certain emotes require purple gems:
- Special animated emotes (complex animations)
