## Understanding the Medal System in Super Bear Adventure

Medals are one of the core progression systems in Super Bear Adventure, rewarding players for accomplishing specific challenges in each world. Whether you are a casual player looking to earn a few extra rewards or a completionist aiming for 100% game completion, understanding how medals work is essential.

The medal system was introduced in version 6.0.0 and has been expanded with each subsequent [game update](/blog/version-changelogs/). Today, there are dozens of medals to earn across all worlds, each testing a different skill or encouraging thorough exploration.

If you are just getting started with Super Bear Adventure, we recommend reading our [beginner guide](/blog/how-to-play-beginner-guide/) first to make sure you have a solid grasp of the game's basic mechanics before pursuing medals.

## Medal Types and Tiers

### Medal Tiers

Each challenge in Super Bear Adventure awards medals in three tiers:

#### Bronze Medal

- The easiest tier to achieve
- Typically requires completing the basic objective
- Generous time limits for timed challenges
- Good starting point for casual players
- Rewards: Small coin bonus and bronze badge

#### Silver Medal

- Moderate difficulty
- Requires better performance than bronze
- Tighter time limits or higher completion thresholds
- Rewards: Medium coin bonus, silver badge, and sometimes cosmetic items

#### Gold Medal

- The highest tier and most difficult to achieve
- Requires near-perfect performance
- Very tight time limits or 100% completion requirements
- Rewards: Large coin bonus, gold badge, and exclusive cosmetics or [skins](/blog/unlock-all-skins-hats/)

### Medal Categories

Medals fall into several categories based on the type of challenge:

#### Completion Medals

Awarded for finding all collectibles in a specific area. This includes coins, [stickers](/blog/all-secret-stickers-locations/), hidden items, and other world-specific collectibles. These medals encourage thorough exploration and reward players who check every corner.

#### Time Trial Medals

Awarded for completing specific routes or missions within time limits. Each tier has a different time threshold, with gold requiring exceptional speed and route knowledge. These medals reward players who have mastered the game's movement mechanics.

#### Combat Medals

Awarded for defeating enemies or [bosses](/blog/boss-fight-strategy/) under specific conditions. This might include defeating a boss without taking damage, defeating a certain number of enemies within a time limit, or using specific attack techniques.

#### Exploration Medals

Awarded for discovering [secret areas](/blog/secret-areas-easter-eggs/), reaching specific vantage points, or finding hidden paths. These medals celebrate curiosity and thoroughness.

#### Mission Medals

Tied to the [game's missions](/blog/all-missions-guide/) and awarded for completing mission objectives with various levels of success. Mission medals often have optional objectives that count toward higher tiers.

## Turtletown Medals

Turtletown is the first world and has the most accessible medals, making it an excellent place to learn medal-hunting strategies. For complete world coverage, see our [Turtletown walkthrough](/blog/turtletown-walkthrough/).

### Completion Medals

#### Coin Collector

- **Bronze**: Collect 50% of all coins in Turtletown
- **Silver**: Collect 75% of all coins in Turtletown
- **Gold**: Collect 100% of all coins in Turtletown
- **Tips**: Coins respawn when you re-enter the world, but the medal tracks unique coin locations found. Check underwater areas and behind waterfalls for hidden coin clusters.

#### Shell Seeker

- **Bronze**: Find 5 hidden turtle shells
- **Silver**: Find 10 hidden turtle shells
- **Gold**: Find all 15 hidden turtle shells
- **Tips**: Turtle shells are placed in non-obvious locations. Check behind large rocks, inside hollow trees, and on elevated platforms that require precise jumping to reach.

#### Sticker Album - Turtletown

- **Bronze**: Find 3 stickers in Turtletown
- **Silver**: Find 6 stickers in Turtletown
- **Gold**: Find all 10 stickers in Turtletown
- **Tips**: Stickers are among the hardest collectibles to find. Use our [sticker locations guide](/blog/all-secret-stickers-locations/) for exact positions.

### Time Trial Medals

#### Beach Sprint

- **Bronze**: Complete the beach course in under 3:00
- **Silver**: Complete the beach course in under 2:00
- **Gold**: Complete the beach course in under 1:15
- **Tips**: Use double jumps to cut corners and skip platforming sections. The optimal route hugs the left side of the beach at the start, then cuts through the cave shortcut.

#### Lighthouse Climb

- **Bronze**: Reach the top of the lighthouse in under 2:30
- **Silver**: Reach the top of the lighthouse in under 1:45
- **Gold**: Reach the top of the lighthouse in under 1:00
- **Tips**: Wall jumps are essential for gold. Instead of using the spiral path, chain wall jumps on the interior walls to skip multiple floors.

### Combat Medals

#### Crab Crusher

- **Bronze**: Defeat 10 crabs without taking damage
- **Silver**: Defeat 20 crabs without taking damage
- **Gold**: Defeat 30 crabs without taking damage
- **Tips**: Use ground pound attacks to defeat crabs safely since they cannot hit you during the ground pound animation. Chain attacks between groups for efficient clearing.

#### Guardian's Challenge

- **Bronze**: Defeat the Turtletown boss
- **Silver**: Defeat the boss taking fewer than 5 hits
- **Gold**: Defeat the boss without taking any damage
- **Tips**: Learn the boss's three-phase pattern thoroughly. Phase transitions always begin with the same tell animation, giving you time to reposition.

## Snow Valley Medals

Snow Valley introduces ice physics that make many medals more challenging. Our [Snow Valley secrets guide](/blog/snow-valley-secrets/) covers the world in detail.

### Completion Medals

#### Frozen Treasure Hunter

- **Bronze**: Collect 50% of Snow Valley's coins
- **Silver**: Collect 75% of Snow Valley's coins
- **Gold**: Collect 100% of Snow Valley's coins
- **Tips**: Many coins in Snow Valley are hidden inside breakable ice blocks. Use ground pound near suspicious ice formations to reveal hidden coins. The ice caves contain the highest concentration of easy-to-miss coins.

#### Snowflake Collection

- **Bronze**: Find 5 unique snowflake crystals
- **Silver**: Find 10 unique snowflake crystals
- **Gold**: Find all 15 unique snowflake crystals
- **Tips**: Snowflake crystals glow faintly, making them visible in dark areas. During blizzard conditions, their glow becomes more apparent. Check mountain ledges and inside igloos.

### Time Trial Medals

#### Ice Sliding Sprint

- **Bronze**: Complete the ice slide course in under 2:30
- **Silver**: Complete the ice slide course in under 1:45
- **Gold**: Complete the ice slide course in under 1:10
- **Tips**: Master the ice physics. On ice surfaces, start your turns early because momentum carries you further than on normal terrain. The gold medal route requires using a hidden ramp shortcut halfway through the course.

#### Mountain Peak Rush

- **Bronze**: Reach the summit in under 4:00
- **Silver**: Reach the summit in under 2:45
- **Gold**: Reach the summit in under 1:50
- **Tips**: This is one of the hardest time trials in the game. The gold medal requires knowledge of wall jump shortcuts and specific rock formations that serve as ledge grabs. Practice the route in free exploration before attempting timed runs.

### Combat Medals

#### Wolf Pack Survivor

- **Bronze**: Survive 3 waves of frost wolves
- **Silver**: Survive 5 waves without healing
- **Gold**: Survive all 7 waves without taking damage
- **Tips**: Position yourself with a wall behind you so wolves cannot attack from all directions. The alpha wolf always appears in waves 4 and 7. Prioritize it to cause the pack to scatter.

## Beemothep Desert Medals

The desert world combines exploration challenges with puzzle-solving medals. Full walkthrough in our [Beemothep Desert guide](/blog/beemothep-desert-puzzle/).

### Completion Medals

#### Tomb Raider

- **Bronze**: Discover 3 hidden tomb chambers
- **Silver**: Discover 6 hidden tomb chambers
- **Gold**: Discover all 10 hidden tomb chambers
- **Tips**: Tomb chambers are hidden behind false walls and buried sand mounds. Look for subtle visual cues like slightly different colored stone or sand that seems disturbed. Some chambers require solving environmental puzzles to access.

#### Desert Artifact Collector

- **Bronze**: Find 5 ancient artifacts
- **Silver**: Find 10 ancient artifacts
- **Gold**: Find all 20 ancient artifacts
- **Tips**: Artifacts are scattered across the desert, many in hard-to-reach locations. The Archaeologist Lizard NPC provides hints about artifact locations if you complete his side quests.

### Puzzle Medals

#### Hieroglyphic Decoder

- **Bronze**: Solve 3 hieroglyphic puzzles
- **Silver**: Solve 7 hieroglyphic puzzles
- **Gold**: Solve all 12 hieroglyphic puzzles
- **Tips**: Each hieroglyphic puzzle follows a logic pattern. Look for matching symbols, sequential numbers, or mirror patterns. The puzzles get progressively harder, with later ones requiring information gathered from earlier puzzles.

#### Pyramid Navigator

- **Bronze**: Navigate the pyramid's first floor
- **Silver**: Navigate to the pyramid's third floor
- **Gold**: Navigate to the pyramid's summit
- **Tips**: The pyramid is a massive maze with traps. Memorize trap patterns rather than trying to react to them. Some traps have safe spots where you can pause and observe the next section's timing.

- **Silver**: Explore 75% of the Giant House
- **Gold**: Explore 100% of the Giant House

- **Silver**: Reach the top of the kitchen counter in under 2:00
- **Gold**: Reach the top via the speed route in under 1:00

- **Bronze**: Climb to the top shelf
- **Silver**: Climb to the top shelf collecting all coins
- **Gold**: Climb to the top shelf in under 1:30 collecting all coins

## The Hive Medals

The Hive's medals focus on navigation and combat in its honeycomb structure. Details in our [Hive walkthrough](/blog/the-hive-walkthrough/).

### Navigation Medals

#### Honeycomb Navigator

- **Bronze**: Navigate through 5 honeycomb chambers
- **Silver**: Navigate through 10 honeycomb chambers
- **Gold**: Navigate through all 18 honeycomb chambers
- **Tips**: The honeycomb structure can be disorienting. Use the hexagonal patterns to maintain your sense of direction. Each chamber has six possible exits, but only some are real paths.

### Combat Medals

#### Royal Guard Challenge

- **Bronze**: Defeat the Royal Guard boss
- **Silver**: Defeat the Royal Guard in under 5:00
- **Gold**: Defeat the Royal Guard without taking damage in under 3:00
- **Tips**: The Royal Guard has three phases. In phase one, dodge lance thrusts and counterattack. Phase two adds aerial attacks that require precise dodging. Phase three combines both with drone reinforcements that you must manage simultaneously.

## General Tips for Medal Hunting

### Prioritize Exploration First

Before attempting any timed or combat medals, thoroughly explore each world in free play mode. Knowing the layout, enemy positions, and hidden paths is essential for achieving gold medals. This exploration will also help you discover [secret areas and easter eggs](/blog/secret-areas-easter-eggs/) that might contain medal-related content.

### Practice Movement Mechanics

Many gold medals require advanced movement techniques:

- **Chain jumping**: Jumping immediately upon landing preserves momentum
- **Wall jump cancels**: Canceling a wall jump with a directional input can reach otherwise inaccessible areas
- **Ground pound bouncing**: Ground pounding onto certain surfaces bounces Baaren higher than a normal jump
- **Slide jumping**: Jumping while sliding on slopes gives extra speed and distance

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do medals carry over between game versions?

Yes, your medal progress is saved to your account and carries over through [game updates](/blog/version-changelogs/). New updates may add additional medals but never remove existing ones.

### Can I earn medals in the mod version?

Using the [mod APK](/blog/super-bear-adventure-mod-apk-download/) does not prevent medal earning. However, using certain [mod menu](/blog/super-bear-adventure-mod-menu/) features like invincibility may disqualify specific combat medals that require no-damage completion.

### What are the hardest medals to earn?

The community generally considers these the most difficult gold medals:
1. Mountain Peak Rush (Snow Valley) - extremely tight time requirement
2. Royal Guard No-Damage (The Hive) - demanding combat perfection
3. Perfect Mine Cart Run (Mole Mines) - requires memorization and reflexes
4. Pyramid Summit (Beemothep Desert) - complex navigation under pressure

### Can I reset my medal progress?

Medal progress cannot be selectively reset. If you want to re-earn medals, you would need to start a new save file. This is generally not recommended since medal progress represents significant time investment.
