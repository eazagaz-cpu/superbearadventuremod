Super Bear Adventure has a growing speedrunning community. This guide covers everything you need — category definitions, major skips, world-by-world routing, and how to get started submitting runs.

## Speedrun Categories

### Any% (Most Popular)

Complete the game from start to credits as fast as possible. Any% allows any method including out-of-bounds, clipping, and skip glitches as long as the ending is reached.

**Current estimated top time:** Sub-25 minutes for optimized routes.

### All Worlds

Complete all 6 worlds (clear the final mission in each) without necessarily 100% completing them. This category demands deeper knowledge of each world's mission layout.

### 100%

Complete every mission, collect all stickers, defeat all bosses, and reach 100% world completion in all 6 worlds. The longest category — estimated top runs around 3–5 hours.

### Co-op Any%

New category since v12.0.0. Two-player co-op to complete the game as fast as possible. Some skips are easier in co-op (one player can set up while the other acts). New strategies are still being discovered.

## Major Skips and Tricks

### 1. Turtletown Fence Clip (Saves ~3 minutes)

**Location:** Near the first checkpoint in Turtletown, there is a wooden fence.

**Technique:** Jump at the fence from a specific angle (approach from the left side at approximately 45 degrees) while pressing forward. Baaren clips through the fence geometry into the out-of-bounds area behind the tutorial section.

**Effect:** Skips the entire first tutorial zone, reaching the beach area directly. Saves approximately 3 minutes in Any%.

**Difficulty:** Medium — requires consistent jump angle. Takes 15–30 minutes to learn.

### 2. Snow Valley Ice Slide (Saves ~90 seconds)

**Location:** The frozen lake in Snow Valley's second area.

**Technique:** Enter the ice at maximum running speed from a specific corner. Baaren enters a perpetual slide state without the standard deceleration. Traverse the lake in 8 seconds instead of 35.

**Effect:** Skips the slow careful-walking section across ice. Saves approximately 90 seconds.

**Difficulty:** Easy to medium — requires knowing the entrance position. Consistent after ~10 attempts.

### 3. Beemothep Desert Pyramid Clip (Saves ~2 minutes)

**Location:** The main pyramid structure in Beemothep Desert.

**Technique:** Jump at the west corner of the pyramid's base at a specific rhythm (jump, land, jump). A geometry gap in the wall allows clipping inside the pyramid, skipping the exterior puzzle sequence.

**Effect:** Allows entry to the final interior section directly. Saves approximately 2 minutes.

**Difficulty:** Hard — requires precise timing and positioning. This is the most technical skip.

### 4. Giant House Out-of-Bounds Shortcut (Saves ~4 minutes)

**Location:** The bathroom area on the second floor of the Giant House.

**Technique:** Stand on the toilet seat and jump toward the wall at a specific angle. The bathroom wall has a thin geometry gap at its top edge that allows exiting to the exterior of the house.

**Effect:** Access the final attic area directly, skipping the kitchen-to-living room-to-staircase sequence. Saves approximately 4 minutes — the largest single skip in the game.

**Difficulty:** Hard — wall exit requires multiple jump attempts.

### 5. The Hive Elevator Skip (Saves ~60 seconds)

**Location:** The vertical elevator shaft connecting floor 2 to floor 3 of The Hive.

**Technique:** A flying enemies' collision box can be used as a platform. Stand on the enemy as it moves upward, then jump to the floor 3 ledge without riding the elevator.

**Effect:** Skips elevator wait time. Saves approximately 60 seconds.

**Difficulty:** Medium — depends on enemy AI timing, which varies slightly.

### 6. Mole Mines Cart Trick (Saves ~2 minutes)

**Location:** The mine cart section in the first tunnel of Mole Mines.

**Technique:** Exit the mine cart at the first split track point by pressing jump + left simultaneously. Land on a hidden ledge above the track. Follow the ledge to emerge at the post-cart checkpoint.

**Effect:** Skips the full mine cart ride. Saves approximately 2 minutes.

**Difficulty:** Medium — timing the exit is specific but learnable.

## World-by-World Routing (Any%)

### Turtletown

1. Start game
2. Collect just enough coins to look legitimate (optional — skip if not caring about anti-ban)
3. Fence clip at checkpoint 1
4. Reach the beach boss trigger
5. Turtle King fight — use the shell vulnerability on attack 3 (skip phases 1 and 2)
6. World clear

**Estimated time:** 6–8 minutes with fence clip.

### Snow Valley

1. Enter via world select
2. Ice slide skip on frozen lake
3. Standard path through snow caves
4. Frost Giant — attack the exposed back during the spinning phase
5. World clear

**Estimated time:** 5–7 minutes.

### Beemothep Desert

1. Pyramid clip at western base
2. Access interior final section
3. Sand Pharaoh fight — hit the sarcophagus gem during the summon animation
4. World clear

**Estimated time:** 7–9 minutes without clip; 5–7 minutes with clip.

### Giant House

1. Standard path through entrance
2. OOB shortcut in bathroom (floor 2)
3. Access attic directly
4. House Cat — attack during the yarn ball phase exclusively
5. World clear

**Estimated time:** 6–8 minutes with OOB shortcut.

### The Hive

1. Ground floor standard path
2. Elevator skip on floor 2→3
3. Honey maze standard run
4. Queen Bee — hit stinger during phase 2 hover only
5. World clear

**Estimated time:** 7–9 minutes.

### Mole Mines

1. Enter mine cart
2. Cart trick exit at split track
3. Standard path through crystal caverns
4. Mole King — attack during the crystal-throw animation
5. World clear

**Estimated time:** 8–10 minutes with cart trick.

## Total Any% Estimated Time

With all major skips performed:
- Turtletown: ~7 min
- Snow Valley: ~6 min
- Desert: ~6 min
- Giant House: ~7 min
- The Hive: ~8 min
- Mole Mines: ~9 min
- Loading/transitions: ~4 min

**Total: approximately 47 minutes** for a clean run with all skips. Optimized play can push under 35 minutes.

## Getting Started

### For Beginners

Start with Any% without any skips. Learn the optimal path through each world first. Add skips one by one as you become comfortable with each world's layout.

The Snow Valley ice slide is the easiest skip — learn it first.

### Recording Runs

Use a screen recorder (built into Android) to capture your run. Most categories require video proof for leaderboard submission.

### Timer

Use a split timer app on a second device, or time your full run from video. RTA (real time) timing starts from first input on the opening screen.
