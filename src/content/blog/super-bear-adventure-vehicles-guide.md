Super Bear Adventure includes six distinct vehicles spread across its six worlds. Each vehicle has unique controls and is used in specific missions. This guide covers every rideable — where to find it, how to control it, and any known tricks.

## Complete Vehicle List

### 1. Scooter — Turtletown

**World:** Turtletown  
**Location:** Near the village market, parked beside the delivery stall  
**Used in:** Delivery missions in Turtletown

**Controls:**
- Left/Right: Steer
- Jump button: Hop (small hop, can mount curbs)
- Brake: Hold down while moving

**Tips:**
- The scooter is slower than running but required for specific delivery missions
- Stay on the main roads — the scooter handles poorly off-road
- Delivery timer missions (if any) require knowing the optimal route between delivery points

**Speedrun note:** The scooter delivery mission is one of the longer missions in Turtletown. Knowing the route in advance saves 30–60 seconds.

---

### 2. Sailboat — Turtletown Beach / Water Areas

**World:** Turtletown (beach section) and water areas in other worlds  
**Location:** Docked at the Turtletown beach marina  
**Used in:** Water crossing missions, ocean exploration

**Controls:**
- Left/Right: Steer direction
- Forward: Move forward automatically (press once to engage)
- Jump: Small hop (used to avoid waves)
- Dash: Speed boost (short duration)

**Tips:**
- The dash speed boost is activated by quickly pressing Forward twice. This is useful for racing missions
- The sailboat supports 2 players simultaneously in co-op — both players can ride on the same boat
- Hitting the dock at high speed causes a bounce effect that can launch Baaren off the boat

**Special technique — Sail skip:** On open water sections without mission triggers, the speed boost dash can be chained continuously to cross water areas significantly faster than normal sailing speed.

---

### 3. Glider — Snow Valley

**World:** Snow Valley  
**Location:** At the top of the first major cliff in Snow Valley, next to a launch pad  
**Used in:** Long-distance gap crossing, cliff descent, aerial exploration

**Controls:**
- Left/Right: Steer direction
- Forward/Back: Pitch (descend faster or slower)
- Jump button while on glider: Dismount (launches Baaren forward from glider)

**Tips:**
- The glider uses updraft currents — fly over visually distinct smoke/mist areas on the ground for altitude gain
- The glider provides the best aerial view of Snow Valley, useful for spotting sticker locations
- Glider dismount mid-air allows you to transition directly to Baaren's double jump for tricky precision landings

**Speedrun note:** The Snow Valley glider can reach areas that walking would take much longer to navigate. Knowing the updraft locations is key to glider speedrunning.

---

### 4. Desert Train — Beemothep Desert

**World:** Beemothep Desert  
**Location:** The desert train station on the western side of Beemothep  
**Used in:** Traversal across the wide desert sections, specific relic collection mission

**Controls:**
- Automatic movement (train follows rails)
- Left/Right: Lean to affect speed (lean forward = faster)
- Jump: Hop on/off the train
- Interact while on train: Trigger specific events (in mission)

**Tips:**
- The desert train has fixed stops — you cannot control its path
- Some collectibles (stickers) are visible from the train and can be grabbed by jumping off at the right moment
- The train is required for the main desert traversal mission and is optional for most other exploration

**Hidden feature:** At the final train stop, if you jump onto the roof of the station building before the train departs, you can access a hidden sticker that is otherwise very difficult to reach.

---

### 5. Bee Car — Giant House

**World:** Giant House  
**Location:** In the Giant House kitchen, parked beside the refrigerator  
**Used in:** Kitchen navigation mission, living room exploration

**Controls:**
- Left/Right: Steer
- Forward: Drive forward
- Jump: Hop (can jump onto countertops)
- Reverse: Hold back while at low speed

**Tips:**
- The Bee Car is sized appropriately for Baaren but is still quite large — some narrow passages require exiting the car
- The hop jump allows climbing onto kitchen counters, which is required for specific missions
- Multiple players can ride in the Bee Car in co-op (one front, one back)

**Unique mechanic:** The Bee Car's horn (press Interact while driving) triggers certain NPC reactions in the Giant House kitchen, which unlocks a hidden dialogue sequence.

---

### 6. Mine Cart — Mole Mines

**World:** Mole Mines  
**Location:** Available at the mine cart station at the start of Mole Mines  
**Used in:** Main traversal through the mine tunnel sections (required for main path)

**Controls:**
- Automatic forward movement
- Jump: Switch tracks at split points (jump right before the split)
- Duck: Avoid low-hanging stalactites
- Lean: Minor speed adjustment on inclines

**Tips:**
- Track switches happen fast — recognize the split point well in advance
- Jumping too early or too late at a split results in staying on the same track
- Some tracks lead to dead ends (dead-end tracks have extra coins but no exit — you loop back)
- The highest-gem-yield route in the mine cart takes the right track at split 2 and the left at split 4

**Speedrun trick — Cart Exit:** Exit the mine cart at split 1 by pressing Jump + Left simultaneously. This lands on a hidden ledge that bypasses the entire cart ride and emerges at the post-cart checkpoint. See the [speedrunning guide](/blog/speedrunning-super-bear-adventure/) for exact timing.

## Vehicles in Multiplayer

In co-op sessions, vehicles work as follows:

- **Scooter, Glider, Mine Cart, Desert Train:** Each player has their own instance. Multiple players can use vehicles simultaneously in the same area.
- **Sailboat, Bee Car:** Supports 2 players on the same vehicle. Player 1 controls, Player 2 rides.

For co-op setup, see the [multiplayer guide](/blog/multiplayer-guide/).
