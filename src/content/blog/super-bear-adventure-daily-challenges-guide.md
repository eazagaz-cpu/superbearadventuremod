Daily challenges are Super Bear Adventure's most efficient coin and gem source. This guide covers every challenge type, the best strategies for each, and how to maintain streaks for maximum rewards.

## How the Daily Challenge System Works

Every day at **midnight (local time)**, Super Bear Adventure refreshes with 3 new daily challenges. The challenges are:

- **Pre-generated** — not fully random, chosen from a rotating pool of challenge types
- **Difficulty-scaled** — three tiers: Easy, Medium, Hard (each day has one of each)
- **Individually rewarding** — each challenge completed gives its own reward
- **Bonus-rewarding** — completing all 3 adds a full-completion bonus

The challenge pool includes missions across all 6 worlds, Arcade World tasks, combat challenges, collection challenges, and multiplayer objectives.

## Challenge Types Explained

### Collection Challenges

**Example:** "Collect 300 coins in Snow Valley"

**Strategy:** These are generally the fastest. Use the coin farming route for the specified world. Snow Valley's coin trail around the frozen lake is particularly efficient.

**Mod tip:** The collectible highlight in the mod menu makes coin trails more visible. Speed multiplier allows faster traversal of collection routes.

**Average completion time:** 3–5 minutes.

### Combat Challenges

**Example:** "Defeat 15 enemies in The Hive"

**Strategy:** Enter an area with high enemy density. The Hive's worker bee corridors and Mole Mines' main tunnels have the highest enemy concentration in the game.

**Mod tip:** God mode removes death risk. One-hit enemies (mod menu) makes combat faster.

**Average completion time:** 5–8 minutes.

### Mission Challenges

**Example:** "Complete the Pyramid Interior Puzzle"

**Strategy:** These require completing a specific mission. If it is a mission you have not yet done, this is an efficient push. If you have done it, replay via the mission select screen.

**Note:** Some mission challenges require specific world states. If a boss has not been defeated yet, certain missions may not be available.

**Average completion time:** 8–15 minutes (depends on mission length).

### Time Trial Challenges

**Example:** "Reach the summit of Snow Valley in under 4 minutes"

**Strategy:** These are the most demanding. Know the optimal route in advance. The [speedrunning guide](/blog/speedrunning-super-bear-adventure/) covers fast routes for each world.

**Mod tip:** Speed multiplier (moderate setting, 1.5–2x) gives you additional buffer on tight time trials without making control unwieldy.

**Average completion time:** Varies — budget extra time for retries.

### Multiplayer Challenges

**Example:** "Complete any world in co-op with a friend"

**Strategy:** If you have a friend who plays, these are quick. If not, use two devices or BlueStacks multi-instance. See the [multiplayer guide](/blog/multiplayer-guide/).

**Note:** These challenges have the highest reward payouts because they require another player.

**Average completion time:** 10–20 minutes (includes lobby setup time).

### Arcade World Challenges

**Example:** "Score 1000 points in the ball game"

**Strategy:** Arcade World challenges have consistent techniques. See the [Arcade World guide](/blog/arcade-world-mini-games/) for high score strategies.

**Average completion time:** 5–10 minutes.

## Reward Breakdown

### Individual Challenge Rewards

| Difficulty | Coin Reward | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Easy | 50–100 coins | Typically collection or short mission |
| Medium | 100–200 coins | Combat or standard mission |
| Hard | 200–300 coins | Time trial or multiplayer |

### Full Completion Bonus

Completing all 3 challenges earns a bonus that scales with total difficulty:

- **All Easy day:** ~100 coins + 10 purple gems
- **Mixed day:** ~250 coins + 20 purple gems
- **All Hard day:** ~400 coins + 30 purple gems

### Streak Bonuses

Consecutive-day completion adds bonuses:

| Streak | Bonus |
|--------|-------|
| 3 days | +50 coins on completion |
| 7 days | +100 coins + 15 extra purple gems |
| 30 days | +500 coins + 50 gems + exclusive cosmetic |

**Note:** Streaks require completing all 3 challenges daily. Missing one challenge breaks the streak.

## Optimal Daily Challenge Routine

### Morning Checklist

1. Open the game
2. Go to Daily Challenges menu
3. Read all 3 challenges
4. Plan: which world/task is each in?
5. Group by location (do Turtletown challenges together)
6. Start with Medium → Easy → Hard (or Easy → Medium → Hard for stamina)
7. Claim each reward as you complete

### Time Budget

With practice, completing 3 daily challenges takes:

- Easy + Easy + Easy: 10–15 minutes
- Mixed set (Easy/Medium/Hard): 20–30 minutes
- Hard multiplayer challenge day: 30–45 minutes

### When to Use the Mod Menu

Time trial challenges are the main case. Set the speed multiplier to 1.5x for time trial challenges to get comfortable buffer room without losing precise control.
