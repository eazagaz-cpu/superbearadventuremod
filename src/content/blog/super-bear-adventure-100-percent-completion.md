100% completion in Super Bear Adventure requires clearing every mission, collecting every sticker, and finding every secret in all 6 worlds. This guide provides the complete checklist for v12.1.5.

## What Counts Toward 100%

Super Bear Adventure tracks completion per world. For each world to show 100%:

1. **All story missions completed** (main progression)
2. **All side missions completed** (optional but tracked)
3. **All stickers collected** (hidden collectibles)
4. **World boss defeated** (required for progress)
5. **Hidden secrets found** (world-specific)

The overall game percentage is calculated as an average across all 6 worlds.

## Cross-World Completion Tips

### Order of Completion

The optimal completion order follows the world unlock sequence:

1. Turtletown → 2. Snow Valley → 3. Desert → 4. Giant House → 5. The Hive → 6. Mole Mines

See the [worlds overview](/worlds/) for context on how each world connects.

### Sticker Hunting Tips

- Activate the mod menu's collectible highlight to see stickers through walls and terrain
- Check all vertical spaces — stickers are frequently placed on ceilings and atop structures
- Water and underground areas often hide stickers that players miss on first pass
- Check behind waterfalls, inside hollow objects, and above doorways

### Boss Rematches

Boss rematches are available after the initial defeat. For 100% you only need one clear. Rematches are relevant for [coin farming](/blog/unlimited-coins-farming/) and the [gems guide](/blog/super-bear-adventure-unlimited-gems/) but not required for completion.

## Daily Challenges and Achievements

While daily challenges are not part of the world completion percentage, some in-game achievements require:

- 10 consecutive daily challenge completions
- Completing 30 total daily challenges (cumulative)
- Winning in Super Bear Fighters

These achievements unlock special cosmetics. See the [daily challenges guide](/blog/super-bear-adventure-daily-challenges-guide/).
