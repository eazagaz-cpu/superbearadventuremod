Packed with quirky characters, cleverly hidden secrets, and satisfying platforming challenges, this coastal village rewards thorough exploration like no other area. ## How to Access Turtletown

Turtletown becomes accessible after completing the introductory missions in Bearhaven:

1. Open your world map from the Bearhaven hub area.
2. Look for the Turtletown icon, resembling a coastal village with a shell-shaped landmark.
3. Select it and confirm travel.
4. You spawn at the Turtletown entrance gate near the beach boardwalk.

No prerequisite missions are required beyond the initial tutorial. Check our [complete missions guide](/blog/all-missions-guide/) for a recommended world order.


## Turtletown Overview

Turtletown is a medium-sized world set along a tropical coastline, divided into six zones:

- **The Boardwalk** -- Starting area with shops, NPCs, and the mission board.
- **The Beach** -- Sandy stretch with hidden caves beneath the cliffs.
- **The Harbor** -- Boats, docks, and underwater access points.
- **The Village Center** -- Where turtle NPCs live and offer side quests.
- **The Lighthouse Cliffs** -- Elevated platforming sections with panoramic views.
- **The Coral Caves** -- Underground network accessible from the beach and harbor.

Each zone connects through paths, ladders, and shortcuts you unlock as you progress.


## Main Missions Walkthrough

Turtletown contains five main missions. Here is a step-by-step breakdown.

### Mission 1: The Missing Shell Collection

**Objective:** Help Elder Tortuga recover five stolen shells around the boardwalk.

1. Speak to Elder Tortuga near the large fountain at the boardwalk's far end.
2. Shell 1 is behind the ice cream stand. Shell 2 sits on the boardwalk railing near the entrance.
3. Shell 3 is under the boardwalk -- drop down from the side to find it. Shell 4 is inside the barrel next to the fishing supply shop.
4. Shell 5 is on top of the stacked crates near the harbor entrance.
5. Return all five to Elder Tortuga for your coin reward.

### Mission 2: Harbor Havoc

**Objective:** Clear the harbor of crabs and repair the damaged dock.

1. Head to the harbor and defeat three waves of crabs along the dock.
2. Speak to Captain Shellsworth, who asks for three driftwood planks from the beach.
3. Find them along the shoreline: one buried in sand near the rock formation, one floating near the cave entrance, one against the cliff wall.
4. Deliver the driftwood to restore the dock and gain boat access.

### Mission 3: Coral Cave Expedition

**Objective:** Navigate the Coral Caves and retrieve the ancient turtle artifact.

1. Enter through the beach entrance at the base of the southern cliff.
2. At the fork in the second chamber, take the left path first through a platforming section to find a lever.
3. Pull the lever, return to the fork, and take the right path through the now-open gate.
4. The artifact sits on a pedestal in a large cavern. **Before grabbing it**, collect the two stickers and coin cache in this room -- they become inaccessible once you trigger the 60-second flood escape.
5. Grab the artifact and follow the glowing ground markers to escape.

### Mission 4: Lighthouse Restoration

**Objective:** Climb the lighthouse cliffs and restore power to the lighthouse.

The climb has five vertical sections with increasing difficulty: basic ledges, swinging rope platforms, crumbling ledges (sprint across these), wind gusts combined with moving platforms, and a final section combining all elements. At the top, interact with the mechanism to trigger a cutscene revealing a hidden harbor path.

### Mission 5: The Turtle King's Trial

**Objective:** Complete the Turtle King's three-part challenge.

1. Enter the palace (accessible after Missions 1-4) and speak to the Turtle King.
2. **Part 1:** Combat arena with enemy waves. Use pillars for cover.
3. **Part 2:** Timed obstacle course through the palace gardens.
4. **Part 3:** Puzzle room -- arrange turtle statues on pressure plates matching the floor mosaic pattern.
5. Completing all parts unlocks the boss encounter.


## All Secret Areas

Turtletown hides seven secret areas:

1. **The Smuggler's Cove** -- Swim under the repaired harbor dock and find the underwater tunnel on the west side. Contains 200 coins.
2. **The Rooftop Garden** -- Climb the tallest village building using back-wall vines. Holds a sticker and 50 coins.
3. **The Sunken Ship** -- Dive underwater past the harbor dock toward the rock formation. The ship hull contains coins and a cosmetic item.
4. **The Waterfall Alcove** -- Walk through the waterfall in the Coral Caves' second chamber for a hidden chest.
5. **The Lighthouse Basement** -- Return after Mission 4 to find an unlocked door with a lore journal entry. See our [Baaren lore guide](/blog/lore-baaren-story/) for context.
6. **The Tide Pool Grotto** -- Walk to the beach's far eastern edge during low tide to reveal a hidden grotto with a rare sticker.
7. **The Palace Secret Room** -- After Mission 5, interact with the puzzle room's center statue to reveal a hidden staircase with coins and a lore scroll.

For secrets across all worlds, check our [secret areas and easter eggs guide](/blog/secret-areas-easter-eggs/).


## Hidden Sticker Locations

Turtletown contains eight hidden stickers:

1. **Boardwalk** -- Behind the entrance sign.
2. **Beach** -- Atop the large rock formation at the east end.
3. **Harbor** -- Inside the captain's cabin on the docked boat (after Mission 2).
4. **Coral Cave 1** -- On a ledge above the artifact pedestal. Collect before grabbing the artifact.
5. **Coral Cave 2** -- In a wall crack behind the artifact pedestal.
6. **Village** -- On the tallest building's rooftop (Secret Area 2).
7. **Lighthouse** -- Hidden ledge behind the moving platforms in Section 3. Jump backward from the third platform.
8. **Palace** -- In the palace secret room (Secret Area 7), next to the lore scroll.

## Boss Fight: The Coral Guardian

After Mission 5, board the harbor boat to sail to the boss island.

**Phase 1:** The Guardian uses sweeping claw strikes. Dodge sideways and hit from behind. Three hits advance the phase.

**Phase 2:** It summons crab minions and shields with coral armor. Clear the minions first (they drop health), then hit the exposed Guardian twice.

**Phase 3:** The Guardian charges and ground pounds. Jump over shockwaves and attack during recovery. Four hits end the fight.

## Shortcuts and Tips

- **Boardwalk to Harbor** -- Jump off the railing near the ice cream stand and follow the shoreline right.
- **Village to Lighthouse** -- Use the vine wall behind the Turtle King's palace to skip the winding path.
- **Coral Cave Quick Exit** -- After Mission 3, a new tunnel in the first chamber leads to the beach.
- **Rooftop Route** -- After finding Secret Area 2, travel across village rooftops to reach the palace quickly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I replay missions?

Yes. Speak to the original mission giver to replay any completed mission. Your collectible progress is preserved, but you can farm coins and practice sections.

### What if I miss the Coral Cave stickers?

The two stickers in the artifact room must be collected before grabbing the artifact. If you miss them, replay Mission 3 to try again.

### How many total coins are in Turtletown?

A thorough playthrough yields approximately 2,500 coins before farming.

### The lighthouse platforming is too hard. Any tips?

Section 4 with wind gusts is the hardest part. Jump at the peak of each platform's swing and angle slightly into the wind. Our [troubleshooting guide](/blog/troubleshooting-common-errors/) covers additional gameplay issues.
