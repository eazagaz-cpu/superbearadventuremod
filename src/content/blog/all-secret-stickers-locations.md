## What Are Secret Stickers?

Secret stickers are one of the most sought-after collectibles in Super Bear Adventure. There are 15 stickers hidden across every world in the game, and collecting all of them unlocks the exclusive Golden Bear skin, one of the rarest cosmetics available. Unlike coins and gems that are scattered abundantly throughout levels, stickers are carefully hidden in secret areas, behind puzzles, and in locations that most players walk right past.

Each sticker is a unique design featuring characters, items, and scenes from the game's lore. They appear as small glowing cards floating in the air, and collecting one triggers a brief animation showing the sticker being added to your collection book. You can view your collected stickers in the pause menu under the "Collection" tab.

If you are just getting started with Super Bear Adventure, check out our [beginner guide](/blog/how-to-play-beginner-guide/) to learn the basic mechanics before hunting for stickers. Many sticker locations require advanced movement techniques like wall jumping, precise platforming, or knowledge of hidden mechanics.

## Sticker Collection Overview

The 15 stickers are distributed across the game's worlds as follows:

- **Overworld Hub**: 2 stickers
- **Turtletown**: 2 stickers
- **Snow Valley**: 2 stickers
- **Beemothep Desert**: 2 stickers
- **The Hive**: 2 stickers
- **Mole Mines**: 2 stickers
- **Giant House**: 2 stickers
- **Arcade World**: 1 sticker

Each world section below provides the exact location, how to reach it, and any prerequisites needed.

## Overworld Hub Stickers

### Sticker 1: Behind the Waterfall

**Location**: The main waterfall near the center of the overworld hub.

**How to Find It**: From the spawn point, head north toward the large waterfall that flows into the river. Instead of following the river, walk directly into the waterfall itself. Behind the water curtain, there is a narrow ledge leading to a small cave. The sticker floats at the back of this cave.

**Prerequisites**: None. This sticker is accessible from the very start of the game, making it a great first find.

**Difficulty**: Easy.

### Sticker 2: Top of the Clocktower

**Location**: The clocktower building in the village area of the overworld hub.

**How to Find It**: The clocktower has no obvious way to climb it from the inside. Instead, go to the building next to the clocktower (the item shop) and climb the ladder on its back wall to reach the shop's roof. From the roof, perform a running jump to the clocktower's ledge. Shimmy around the ledge to the back side, where a series of small platforms leads to the top. The sticker is on the very top of the clocktower, next to the clock face.

**Prerequisites**: You need to understand the wall-shimmy mechanic. Hold the direction toward the wall while on a narrow ledge to shimmy along it.

**Difficulty**: Medium.

## Turtletown Stickers

### Sticker 3: Underwater Cave

**Location**: Turtletown's main beach area, underwater.

**How to Find It**: Swim out from the main beach into the ocean. Dive underwater and look for a dark opening in the sea floor near the third coral formation from the shore. Swim down into the opening to enter an underwater cave system. Follow the cave to the right, and you will surface in a small air pocket chamber. The sticker is floating above the water in this chamber.

**Prerequisites**: Familiarity with the underwater swimming controls. Your air meter is not a concern here, as the cave is short enough to reach the air pocket comfortably.

**Difficulty**: Medium.

For the full Turtletown walkthrough including all secrets, see our [Turtletown guide](/blog/turtletown-walkthrough/).

### Sticker 4: Palm Tree Canopy

**Location**: The group of tall palm trees in Turtletown's village area.

**How to Find It**: Near the turtle NPC village, there is a cluster of five tall palm trees growing close together. Climb the shortest palm tree using the grab-and-climb mechanic (jump onto the trunk and hold up). At the top, jump from the first tree to the second, then to the third. The third tree has wider fronds that form a small platform. The sticker sits on this platform, hidden from view at ground level.

**Prerequisites**: Comfortable with the tree-climbing mechanic and tree-to-tree jumping.

**Difficulty**: Medium.

## Snow Valley Stickers

### Sticker 5: Inside the Frozen Lake

**Location**: Snow Valley's large frozen lake.

**How to Find It**: Walk onto the frozen lake and look for a spot where the ice is darker and slightly transparent. This is near the center of the lake, slightly to the left. Ground pound on this dark ice patch three times to crack it open. Fall through the hole into a hidden ice cave beneath the lake. The sticker is in this cave along with a coin bonus.

**Prerequisites**: Ground pound ability. The ice does not break from normal walking or jumping.

**Difficulty**: Easy once you know the location.

For more Snow Valley secrets, visit our [Snow Valley secrets guide](/blog/snow-valley-secrets/).

### Sticker 6: Mountain Peak Secret

**Location**: The highest mountain peak in Snow Valley.

**How to Find It**: The tallest mountain in Snow Valley appears to be just scenery, but it is actually climbable. Start from the back side of the mountain (the side facing away from the main path). A series of narrow ledges and ice platforms create a climbing path that spirals around the mountain. The climb takes about two minutes and includes several tricky jumps across icy platforms that you can slide off. At the absolute peak, the sticker hovers above a small flat area.

**Prerequisites**: Patience and precision platforming. The icy surfaces reduce traction, so tap the directional input rather than holding it.

**Difficulty**: Hard.

## Beemothep Desert Stickers

### Sticker 7: Sphinx Puzzle Reward

**Location**: Inside the Sphinx structure in the Beemothep Desert.

**How to Find It**: The Sphinx has a riddle puzzle. Approach the front of the Sphinx and interact with the stone tablet at its base. The tablet presents three riddles. The answers correspond to items you need to bring from around the desert. Answer one: a red flower (found near the oasis). Answer two: a bone (found near the fossil area). Answer three: a gold coin (any coin from your inventory counts). After solving all three riddles, a chamber opens inside the Sphinx containing the sticker.

**Prerequisites**: Exploration of the Beemothep Desert to find the puzzle items.

**Difficulty**: Medium.

See the [Beemothep Desert puzzle guide](/blog/beemothep-desert-puzzle/) for a full breakdown of all the desert's puzzles.

### Sticker 8: Sand Dune Tunnel

**Location**: A hidden tunnel beneath the largest sand dune in the desert.

**How to Find It**: The largest sand dune is in the northwestern part of the desert map. Walk to the base of its eastern face and look for a small, dark opening partially covered by sand. Crouch to enter the tunnel. Inside, the tunnel winds downward into a small underground chamber decorated with ancient symbols. The sticker is on a pedestal in the center of this chamber.

**Prerequisites**: Crouch ability. The entrance is easy to miss because it blends with the surrounding sand.

**Difficulty**: Easy once located, but finding the entrance is the challenge.

## The Hive Stickers

### Sticker 9: Honeycomb Corridors Breakable Wall

**Location**: The Honeycomb Corridors area of The Hive.

**How to Find It**: In the second set of corridors, look for a wall section that has a slightly different shade of gold compared to its neighbors. Punch this wall three times to break through it, revealing a small room with the sticker and a coin bonus.

**Prerequisites**: None beyond basic combat controls.

**Difficulty**: Easy.

Full details on The Hive's layout and secrets are in our [Hive walkthrough](/blog/the-hive-walkthrough/).

### Sticker 10: Royal Jelly Chamber False Floor

**Location**: The Royal Jelly Chamber in The Hive, on the third platform while spiraling upward.

**How to Find It**: As you ascend the Royal Jelly Chamber, the third hexagonal platform you reach has a tile that is very slightly raised compared to the others. Step on this raised tile to trigger a trapdoor that drops you into a hidden room below. The sticker is in this room along with 75 coins. Use the honey wall inside the room to climb back to the main chamber.

**Prerequisites**: Honey wall climbing ability (learned in The Hive's first area).

**Difficulty**: Medium. Noticing the raised tile requires attention to detail.

## Mole Mines Stickers

### Sticker 11: Upper Tunnels Crystal Cave

**Location**: A side tunnel in the Upper Tunnels of the Mole Mines.

**How to Find It**: In the Upper Tunnels' first large cavern, there is a side tunnel branching to the left that is partially hidden behind a rock formation. Follow this tunnel to reach a small crystal cave. The sticker is floating among the crystal formations.

**Prerequisites**: Defeat the group of digger moles guarding the tunnel entrance.

**Difficulty**: Easy.

See our [Mole Mines walkthrough](/blog/mole-mines-walkthrough/) for a complete guide to this world.

### Sticker 12: Mine Cart Secret Track

**Location**: During the Mine Cart Express section of the Mole Mines.

**How to Find It**: About halfway through the cart ride, during the chasm section, look for a side track branching to the right just before the second gap. Instead of jumping the gap, switch to the right track. This side track leads to a hidden room where the sticker awaits.

**Prerequisites**: Familiarity with the mine cart controls. You need to know the track layout to time the switch correctly. The branch point comes up fast.

**Difficulty**: Hard. The timing is tight and you only get one chance per run.

## Giant House Stickers

### Sticker 13: Inside the Dollhouse

**Location**: The dollhouse on the shelf in the Giant House world.

**How to Find It**: In the Giant House, climb the bookshelf near the window. On the third shelf, there is a dollhouse. Your bear is small enough to enter through the dollhouse's front door. Inside, the dollhouse is a miniature level with a few simple platforming challenges. The sticker is in the dollhouse's attic, reached by climbing the dollhouse's interior stairs.

**Prerequisites**: Ability to reach the third shelf of the bookshelf via platforming.

**Difficulty**: Medium.

For more on the Giant House world, read the [Giant House walkthrough](/blog/giant-house-walkthrough/).

### Sticker 14: Behind the Clock

**Location**: The large wall clock in the Giant House.

**How to Find It**: The giant wall clock in the living room area can be reached by jumping from the mantelpiece. Walk along the narrow ledge above the fireplace and jump to the clock's lower edge. Climb around to the back of the clock (the wall side has a small gap between the clock and the wall). Behind the clock, there is a tiny ledge with the sticker.

**Prerequisites**: Precise jumping and ledge navigation. This is one of the more challenging platforming sticker locations.

**Difficulty**: Hard.

## Arcade World Sticker

### Sticker 15: Perfect Score Reward

**Location**: Awarded as a reward in the Arcade World.

**How to Find It**: This is the only sticker that is not found by exploring a hidden location. Instead, you earn it by achieving a perfect score in any one of the Arcade World's mini-games. The easiest mini-game to perfect is the Whack-a-Mole game, as its patterns are predictable once you memorize them. Achieving a perfect score on any game awards the sticker.

**Prerequisites**: Access to the [Arcade World](/blog/arcade-world-mini-games/) and skill at mini-games.

**Difficulty**: Medium to Hard depending on the mini-game chosen.

## What Happens When You Collect All 15 Stickers

Collecting all 15 stickers completes your sticker collection book and triggers a special cutscene where your bear is awarded the Golden Bear skin. This is one of the most prestigious cosmetics in the game, as it requires thorough exploration of every world. The Golden Bear skin makes your entire bear model a shimmering gold color and adds a subtle sparkle particle effect.

In addition to the skin, completing the collection unlocks the "Sticker Master" achievement and adds a special icon next to your name in [multiplayer mode](/blog/multiplayer-guide/). Other players can see that you have completed the collection, which is a mark of dedication and skill.

## Sticker Hunting Tips

### Use the Sticker Radar

After collecting your fifth sticker, you unlock the Sticker Radar item, which appears as a compass-like indicator on your HUD when you are in a world that has uncollected stickers. The radar pulses faster as you get closer to a sticker, functioning like a hot-cold indicator. This makes finding the remaining stickers much easier.

### Revisit Worlds

You can replay any world at any time from the world select menu. Stickers you have already collected will not appear again, so you can focus on searching new areas without confusion. The world select menu also shows how many stickers you have found in each world versus the total.

### Check Behind Everything

The developers of Super Bear Adventure love hiding stickers behind objects, inside containers, and in areas that require you to look at the environment from unusual angles. Walk behind every large object, enter every doorway, and test every suspicious surface. Breakable walls are common sticker hiding spots, and our [secret areas guide](/blog/secret-areas-easter-eggs/) covers additional hidden areas beyond sticker locations.

### Listen for Audio Cues

When you are near a sticker, a faint chiming sound plays. This audio cue is subtle but distinct from other game sounds. If you hear chiming while exploring, stop and search the immediate area carefully. The sound gets louder as you get closer.
